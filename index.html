About Us

ChoriCheli is a platform dedicated to supporting Nepalese women experiencing domestic abuse in Australia and globally. Our goal is to create a safe space where women can access resources and connect with help without fear of judgment.

We collaborate with NGOs, law firms, and activists to provide the best support possible for our community.

Recent Events

Teej Special Program

We organized the Teej Special Program in Granville, NSW, celebrating our cultural traditions while raising awareness about domestic abuse. The event highlighted the importance of supporting abuse victims and encouraged open discussions about seeking help.

Emergency Helplines in Australia

  • Police, Fire, Ambulance (Life-threatening emergencies): 000
  • National Domestic Violence Helpline (1800RESPECT): 1800 737 732
  • Lifeline (Crisis Support and Suicide Prevention): 13 11 14
  • Beyond Blue (Mental Health Support): 1300 224 636
  • Kids Helpline (For Young People): 1800 551 800
